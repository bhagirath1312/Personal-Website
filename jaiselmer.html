Jaisalmer

The Golden City of India

About Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer, also known as the "Golden City," is a breathtaking destination in Rajasthan, India. Famous for its yellow sandstone architecture, majestic forts, and vast desert landscapes, Jaisalmer is a cultural and historical gem.

Top Attractions

  • Jaisalmer Fort: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the largest forts in the world.
  • Sam Sand Dunes: Experience desert safaris and camel rides in the Thar Desert.
  • Patwon Ki Haveli: A cluster of 19th-century mansions showcasing intricate carvings and architecture.
  • Gadisar Lake: A serene lake surrounded by temples and ghats.
