My Travel Journey

Explore some of the incredible places I've visited and the stories behind them.

Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer, known as the “Golden City,” is a captivating destination located in the heart of the Thar Desert in Rajasthan, India. Renowned for its golden sandstone architecture

Barda Hills

Barda Hills, located near Porbandar in Gujarat, is a picturesque and ecologically rich region. Known locally as Barda Dungar, the area is part of the Barda Wildlife Sanctuary.

Muli

The Muli Swaminarayan Temple and the Madhavrayji Temple are two significant religious landmarks located in Muli, Surendranagar, Gujarat.

Gop

Gop is a small village located near Dwarka in Gujarat, India, known for its religious and cultural significance. It holds a special place in Hindu mythology, especially in connection to Lord Krishna

Khimrana

The temple is primarily dedicated to Lord Shiva, with the main deity being represented in the form of a Shivling. Devotees come to offer prayers and perform rituals to seek divine blessings and spiritual peace.

Chalala

Chalala Apa Dan is a well-known religious tradition or event associated with the town of Chalala in Gujarat. The term Apa Dan refers to a form of charity or donation, typically during religious occasions
