About Khimrana

The Khimrana Mahadev Temple is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in the village of Khimrana, which is situated in the Porbandar district of Gujarat, India. The temple is an important spiritual site for the local community and devotees from nearby areas, attracting pilgrims seeking blessings from Lord Shiva.

Top Attractions

  • Shiv Temple: The temple showcases traditional Hindu architecture, with intricately carved sculptures and a serene ambiance that is perfect for worship and meditation.
